FAQs

Block a Member

To block a user, go to their profile, click the three dots, and then select block member. When a member is blocked, both your communications with that individual and their appearance in matches disappear. Additionally, the person you blocked won't be able to see you anymore.

Unblock a Member

Blocking a user has an eternal effect. You cannot unblock a member after blocking them.

Report a Concern

By going to a user's profile, touching the three dots, and choosing "report a problem," you can submit an issue. Choose the response that most accurately expresses your worry and submit. We prioritize the security of our neighborhoods. Please let us know about rude behavior, offensive photographs, and con artists. All reports are anonymous, and the subject of your complaint will never learn who you are.

Photos and Profiles

How to Upload a Photo

You can upload pictures from Facebook, your device, or a spontaneous selfie.

To upload a photo:

  • Tap the profile icon in the top nav
  • Tap the edit profile icon
  • Tap the add button
  • Select the photo you wish to upload

How to Delete a Photo

  • Tap the profile icon in the top nav
  • Tap the edit profile icon
  • Tap the X button
  • At all times, you must have at least one photo. You will need to upload a new photo in its stead if you try to delete your most recent one.

Why was my Photo Removed?

The requirements for our apps' photos were not met by your shot after assessment. You can submit a fresh image that complies with our requirements. Consider your post carefully. A photo that is improper may be removed from your post.

Photo Dos & Don’ts

Consider your post carefully. A photo that is improper may be removed from your post.

Photo Dos

  • Show off your smile
  • Post recent photos looking your best
  • Show off photos of you doing things you love
  • Include photos of your pets

Photo Don’ts

  • No nude photos
  • No photos depicting violence
  • No copyrighted photos
  • No photos of celebrities

Communication

How to Send a Message

You must slide right to indicate interest in one another before you can send a message. When you find a mutual match, you can start a conversation. Tap the chat icon in the top navigation to start a conversation. Then, tap the person's photo and send a message.

Matches

How to Unmatch a Connection
Long-press on the connection's profile picture and select Unmatch to remove the connection.

My Match Disappeared
It appears that the person may have deleted their account or unpaired with you.

Access and Settings

How to logout

  • Tap the profile icon in the top nav
  • Tap the gear icon to access your settings
  • Tap Logout button to temporarily sign out of the app.
  • Logging out will not hide your profile.

How to Delete my Account

  • Tap the profile icon in the top nav
  • Tap the gear icon to access your settings
  • Tap delete button to completely remove your account
  • Deleting your account will remove all of your connections.

Can I change my phone number?

OuTime allow members to change the phone number associated with their accounts once.
  • Tap the profile icon in the top nav
  • Tap the gear icon to access your settings
  • Tap phone number to change your phone number
  • You can change your phone number once.

Can’t Login / My App Crashed

To avoid being inconvenienced more, come back later. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the OuTime application. Delete the program and then download it again to reinstall. Your matches won't be impacted by this as long as you don't delete your account.
